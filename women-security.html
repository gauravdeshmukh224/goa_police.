About Goa Women Security

Goa Women Security Department is a specialized unit dedicated to the safety, protection, and empowerment of women and girls across the state. The department works under Goa Police and coordinates with various law enforcement agencies to prevent crimes against women.

The department focuses on immediate response to complaints, prevention of harassment, awareness programs, cyber safety assistance, and support services for victims of violence. Its mission is to create a safe and secure environment where women can live, work, and travel freely.

Key Services & Responsibilities

Emergency Response Team

Rapid response units for handling cases of harassment, assault, domestic violence, and public safety concerns.

Women Helpline Support

24/7 helpline assistance providing confidential support and immediate police coordination.

Cyber Crime Protection

Handling online harassment, cyberstalking, blackmail, and digital exploitation cases.

Self-Defense Training

Organizing self-defense workshops and awareness programs in schools and colleges.

Legal & Counseling Support

Guidance and assistance to victims in filing complaints and accessing counseling services.

Public Safety Patrol

Regular patrols in tourist areas, markets, public transport, and educational institutions.

Mission & Vision

The mission of Goa Women Security Department is to eliminate violence against women through strong law enforcement, awareness initiatives, and community participation. The vision is to build a safe Goa where women feel secure and empowered at all times.
