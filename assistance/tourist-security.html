Personal & Property Safety
  • Carry wallet in inside jacket or side trouser pocket, never rear pocket.
  • Hold pocketbooks close to your body. Wrap straps securely.
  • Keep handbags on your lap while dining, not on chair backs.
  • Secure valuables, jewelry and passports in hotel safe deposit boxes.
  • Do not leave luggage unattended at airports or taxi stands.
  • Only give luggage to authorized hotel bell staff and take receipt.
  • Do not leave belongings unattended anywhere.
  • Keep valuables in hotel lockers or bank vaults.
Hotel & Meeting Precautions
  • Do not open hotel room door to unknown persons.
  • Verify unsolicited room service or maintenance with front desk.
  • Research before meeting potential clients; meet in public places.
Public Rules & Legal Guidelines in Goa
  • Public smoking allowed only in designated zones.
  • Alcohol consumption in public places is prohibited.
  • No littering, spitting, or throwing garbage in public areas.
  • Cooking in open public places is unsafe and not advisable.
  • No public urination/defecation. Use public toilets.
  • Loud music after 10:00 PM is not permitted.
  • Use of plastics is banned in Goa.
  • No squatting on footpaths.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Maintain decent dress in religious places.
  • Nudity in public places and beaches is punishable.
  • Exhibitionism in residential areas should be avoided.
Driving & Road Safety
  • Wear helmet when riding two wheelers.
  • Wear seat belt in four wheelers.
  • Do not use mobile phone while driving.
  • Follow lane driving and park only at designated places.
  • Do not drive vehicles on beaches.
Crime Prevention & Social Awareness
  • Beware of touts. Deal only with authorized agents.
  • Do not buy or sell drugs. NDPS Act provisions are strict.
  • Report drug peddlers to nearest Police Station.
  • Do not encourage beggars.
  • Eve-teasing or stalking invites legal action.
  • Do not indulge in illegal activities.
  • Respect children. Paedophilia is a serious punishable offence.
Beach & Sea Safety
  • Do not venture into sea at night, especially after alcohol.
  • Follow instructions of Drishti Lifeguards & Tourist Police.
  • Do not drink alcohol on beaches.
  • Do not break or throw glass bottles on beaches.
