General Driving Rules
  • Always drive on the left side of the road.
  • Obey all traffic signals, road signs and lane markings.
  • Wear seat belts while driving or riding in a car.
  • Two-wheeler riders must wear helmets (driver and pillion).
  • Do not use mobile phones while driving.
  • Maintain safe distance from vehicles ahead.
  • Follow speed limits displayed on roads.
  • Do not drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • Always carry valid Driving License, RC, Insurance and PUC certificate.

Pedestrian & Cyclist Safety
  • Always give priority to pedestrians at zebra crossings.
  • Do not stop vehicles on pedestrian crossings.
  • Cyclists should use reflective gear at night.
  • Slow down near schools, hospitals and residential areas.
Parking Regulations
  • Park only in designated parking zones.
  • Do not park near intersections or sharp turns.
  • Do not block driveways, entrances or emergency exits.
  • Switch off engine when parked.
Highway & Expressway Rules
  • Use indicators while changing lanes.
  • Do not overtake from the left side.
  • Use high beam responsibly at night.
  • Take rest during long journeys to avoid fatigue.
  • Use hazard lights only in emergency situations.
