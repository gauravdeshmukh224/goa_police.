Cyber Crime Prevention Tips

Stay Alert | Stay Secure | Stay Protected

Use Strong Passwords

Create complex passwords with letters, numbers and symbols. Never reuse passwords.

Beware of Phishing

Do not click unknown links or share OTP, bank details via SMS or email.

Secure Your Devices

Install antivirus software and keep your system updated regularly.

Protect Personal Information

Do not overshare personal details on social media platforms.

Avoid Public Wi-Fi

Avoid online banking or financial transactions on public networks.

Report Cyber Crime

Immediately report suspicious online activities to cyber crime authorities.
