CHILDLINE 1098 SERVICE

CHILDLINE stands for a friendly 'didi' or a sympathetic 'bhaiya' who is always there for vulnerable children 24 hours of the day, 365 days of the year. As of March 2014, it has handled millions of calls across India.

CHILDLINE is India's first 24-hour, free, emergency phone service for children in need of aid and assistance. Whether you are a concerned adult or a child, you can dial:
1098
We respond immediately to emergency needs and also link children to long-term care and rehabilitation services.

CHILDLINE is a platform bringing together the Ministry of Women & Child Development, Government of India, Department of Telecommunications, street and community youth, non-profit organisations, academic institutions, the corporate sector and concerned individuals.

Who We Work For

We work for the protection of the rights of all children. Our special focus is on children in need of care and protection, especially vulnerable sections including:

  • Street children and youth living alone on the streets
  • Child labourers in unorganised and organised sectors
  • Child victims of trafficking and exploitation
  • Children abandoned by parents or guardians
  • Missing children
  • Differently-abled children
  • Children affected by conflict and disaster
  • Children whose families are in crisis
