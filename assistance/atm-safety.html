SAFETY TIPS FOR ATM USERS

1. Do Not Allow Strangers to Operate Your ATM Card

If you are not familiar with ATM card usage, do not seek help from strangers at the ATM. Get proper instructions from your bank branch. Never hand over your ATM card to anyone and never disclose your ATM PIN to anybody.

2. Cover Your Password with Your Hand

Hidden cameras may capture your password while you enter it. Always cover the keypad with your hand while typing your PIN to protect your account from unauthorized access.

3. Use Familiar ATMs and Limit Late-Night Visits

Avoid ATMs in dimly lit areas or isolated locations. Prefer ATMs under video surveillance and try to avoid using them late at night.

4. Check Bank Balances Frequently

Regularly check your bank statements and account balance. Report fraud immediately. If fraud is not reported within 60 days, you may face unlimited liability. Sign up for SMS or email alerts to monitor unusual withdrawals.

5. Observe the ATM Carefully

Examine the ATM card slot to ensure it looks legitimate and not tampered with. Watch for anything unusual. Protecting your account is about awareness, paying attention, and understanding the risks. Staying alert always pays.
