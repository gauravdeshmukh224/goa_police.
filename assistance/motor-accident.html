IN CASE OF MOTOR ACCIDENTS

Please follow these important safety guidelines

  • Stop and help the injured. Under Section 134 of the Motor Vehicles Act, it is the responsibility of the driver to take the injured person to the nearest hospital.
  • If the accident involves a vehicle carrying dangerous goods (oil tankers, chemical carriers, etc.), keep everyone away and do not allow smoking nearby.
  • Offer medical assistance. If refused, obtain written confirmation from the injured person.
  • Note down names, addresses of witnesses and registration numbers of vehicles involved.
  • If a police officer arrives, note his name and badge number and provide full details.
  • Do not move the vehicle unless instructed by police or required for safety. If moved, mark tire positions for investigation reference.
  • The victim has the RIGHT to obtain copies of FIR, Panchanama, and Medical Report for compensation claims at the Motor Accident Claim Tribunal.
