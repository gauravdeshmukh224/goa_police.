HOW TO AVOID DEBIT / ATM / CREDIT CARD FRAUD

Social Engineering Alert

Never disclose your card details, PIN, CVV or OTP to anyone over phone or email claiming to be bank officials. Contact your bank directly if your card is blocked.

Timely Delivery of Card & PIN

Ensure you receive your card and PIN on time. If delayed, contact your bank immediately.

Card Protection

Store your card securely and prevent unauthorized access.

Maintain Confidentiality

Keep your PIN, CVV and card details secret. Never write your PIN on the card.

Self Usage Only

Always operate your card yourself. Do not allow others to use it on your behalf.

Safe Terminal Usage

Check ATM/POS machines for suspicious external devices before inserting your card.

Avoid Shoulder Surfing

Cover the keypad while entering your PIN.

Avoid Cloning

Ensure your card is swiped in your presence and never taken out of sight.

Use Secure Websites

Only use SSL-certified websites starting with https and showing a padlock symbol.

Dispose Transaction Slips

Destroy unnecessary transaction slips securely.

Regular Transaction Check

Monitor your transactions regularly and report unauthorized activity immediately.
