About Goa Civil Defence

Goa Civil Defence is an essential volunteer-based organization functioning under the Goa Government to ensure preparedness and effective response during natural and man-made disasters. The department plays a vital role in protecting lives, property, and maintaining public safety during emergencies.

The Civil Defence organization works in coordination with Goa Police, Fire Services, Health Departments, and Disaster Management Authorities to manage crisis situations efficiently. It also conducts awareness programs and training sessions to build disaster-resilient communities.

Key Responsibilities

Disaster Management

Responding to floods, cyclones, earthquakes, and other natural calamities with trained rescue teams and equipment.

Emergency Rescue Operations

Conducting search and rescue missions during accidents, building collapses, and emergency situations.

First Aid & Medical Assistance

Providing immediate medical support and first aid services before advanced medical help arrives.

Public Awareness Programs

Organizing workshops and safety awareness campaigns in schools, colleges, and public institutions.

Volunteer Training

Training citizens in disaster response, evacuation techniques, fire safety, and emergency preparedness.

Community Safety Support

Assisting authorities during major public events, festivals, and crowd management operations.

Training & Capacity Building

Goa Civil Defence regularly conducts certified training programs in disaster response, firefighting techniques, basic life support, CPR, evacuation planning, and risk assessment. Citizens are encouraged to join as volunteers and contribute to building a safer Goa.
