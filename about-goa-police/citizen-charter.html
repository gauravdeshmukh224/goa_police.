GOA POLICE

Citizen Charter

Our commitment to providing transparent, accountable, and citizen-friendly policing services to the people of Goa.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To ensure a safe, secure and peaceful Goa through professional excellence, community partnership, and modern policing practices.

Mission

To uphold the law impartially, protect life and property, prevent crime, and deliver prompt and efficient services to citizens with integrity and respect.

Rights of Citizens

Right to Register FIR

Every citizen has the right to register a First Information Report (FIR) at any police station without delay.

Right to Respectful Treatment

Citizens shall be treated with dignity, fairness, and without discrimination based on caste, religion, gender, or status.

Right to Information

Citizens have the right to receive updates regarding their complaints and legal procedures.

Responsibilities of Citizens

Cooperate with Police

Provide accurate information and assist in investigations when required.

Follow Laws

Respect and obey the laws of the land and maintain public order.

Report Crimes

Immediately inform police authorities about crimes or suspicious activities.

Service Delivery Standards
Service Time Frame
Registration of FIR Immediately upon receiving complaint
Police Verification for Passport Within 7 working days
Character Certificate Within 10 working days
Lost Report Copy Same day
Grievance Redressal

Complaint Mechanism

Citizens can submit complaints directly to the Superintendent of Police or through the official Goa Police website.

Helpline Numbers

Dial 100 for emergency police assistance. Cyber Crime complaints can be reported at the nearest police station or online portal.
