DIRECTOR GENERAL OF POLICE

SHRI ALOK KUMAR, IPS

Goa Police is committed to providing effective, efficient and prompt policing services to the citizens of Goa as well as large number of domestic and international tourists visiting Goa. It shall always strive to fulfill its Mission Statement of "Shanti Seva Nyaya" in true spirit with special focus on women and child safety. Community outreach will continue to be a major bridge between police and citizens.

Accidents and consequent loss of lives are major area of concern. Culture of Road Safety needs to be cultivated through Education and Enforcement. No public authority works in vacuum. Active support of citizens can enhance our effectiveness. I therefore urge citizens to be our eyes and ears. Together we can make Goa a Safe and Secure Paradise.
