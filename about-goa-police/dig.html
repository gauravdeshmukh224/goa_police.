Smt. Varsha Sharma, IPS

Deputy Inspector General of Police
Profile Overview

Smt. Varsha Sharma, IPS, serves as the Deputy Inspector General of Police in Goa Police. She plays a crucial leadership role in overseeing law enforcement operations, supervising senior officers, and ensuring effective implementation of policing strategies across designated zones.

With extensive experience in administration, crime management, and public safety, she is committed to maintaining discipline, transparency, and professional excellence within the police force.

Key Responsibilities

Operational Supervision

Monitoring district policing, crime control operations, and maintaining law & order.

Strategic Planning

Developing policies and strategies to improve public safety and security systems.

Administrative Leadership

Supervising senior officers and ensuring efficient departmental coordination.

Public Interaction

Strengthening police-public relations and encouraging community policing initiatives.

Message from the DIG

“Goa Police remains dedicated to serving the people with integrity, professionalism, and commitment. Our focus is to ensure a safe and secure environment for every citizen while maintaining transparency and accountability.”
