Goa Pink Force

Dedicated Women Police Unit Ensuring Safety, Security & Empowerment of Women and Children Across Goa.

About Goa Pink Force

The Goa Pink Force is a specialized women-centric police unit established by the Goa Police Department to enhance the safety and security of women and children across the state. This dedicated team focuses on preventing crimes against women, responding to emergencies, conducting awareness programs, and ensuring a safe environment for tourists and residents.

The Pink Force officers are specially trained in handling sensitive cases such as harassment, domestic violence, stalking, cybercrime against women, and child safety issues. They operate in public places, schools, colleges, beaches, and tourist hubs to provide immediate assistance and support.

Key Responsibilities & Services

Women Safety Patrol

Regular patrolling in public places, beaches, markets, and tourist areas to prevent harassment and ensure women's safety.

Immediate Emergency Response

Quick response to distress calls and complaints related to harassment, assault, or domestic violence.

Child Protection

Special focus on child safety, preventing trafficking, abuse, and supporting child-friendly policing initiatives.

Cyber Crime Assistance

Help in cases of online harassment, cyberstalking, blackmail, and digital safety awareness for women.

Awareness Programs

Conducting self-defense workshops, safety awareness drives, and educational programs in schools and colleges.

Tourist Support

Ensuring safe travel and assistance to female tourists visiting Goa.
